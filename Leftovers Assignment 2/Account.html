Accounts

Guanhong has many devices and enjoys sharing his designs.
He is looking for an app that allows him to save his creations
on multiple devices without having the need to exporting
exporting and importing his work onto a different platform.
Guanhong downloads FlippingRooms because this app allows him to do so.
He likes this app because the registration form is short and simple,
and he is not required to input a lot of his personal information.
Guanhong is only required to insert his first and last name, username,
email and a password.

1. Guanhong clicks on FlippingRooms app
2. Guanhong clicks on the 'Register'button
3. Guanhong fills in his personal details
4. Guanhong clicks on the '>'button
5. Guanhong successfully registers himself on the app
6. Guanhong clicks on the 'Start remodelling'button
7. Guanhong is able to start a project
8. Guanhong is able to save his projects
